24/01/19

"Snapshot"

Here's an earlier work of mine. Parts of this track sound sleazy and trashy.
Kinda reminds me of that one weird uncle that's always hitting on people at parties.
On the other hand, other parts sound a lot more playful and bouncy.
The juxtaposition as awful and weird as it initially presents, manages to work together well before ending on an epic outro.
